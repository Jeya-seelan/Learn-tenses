Tenses-Forms with example

Tenses are splited into twelve types.They are defined with their forms and examples are given as below in the table.

Tense Form Example
simple Past tense 2nd form Ex : I studied english yesterday
Present tense 1st form + s/es Ex : I study english
Future tense will/shall + 1st form Ex : I will study english
Continuous Past tense was/were + 1st form + ing Ex : I was studying english
Present tense am/is/are + 1st form + ing Ex : I am studying english
Future tense will/shall be + 1st form + ing Ex : I will be studying english
Perfect Past tense had + 3rd form Ex : I had studied english
Present tense has/have + 3rd form Ex : I have studied english
Future tense will have + 3rd form Ex : I will have studied
Perfect continuous Past tense had been + 1st form + ing Ex : I had been studying english
Present tense has/have been + 1st form + ing Ex : I have been studying english
Future tense will been + 1st form + ing Ex : I will have been studying english
